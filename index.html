Why did I do Ironhack?

I choose Ironhack because after for years working at amazon.fr as a webmerchandiser & editorialist I wanted to improve my skills in UI and UX design. I wanted to learn more, about the design thinking and Agile methods, to developp my expertise in user research analysis and to enhance my skills in UI design.

What do you look for in a company culture ?

Most of all in company culture I'm looking for real partnership, team spirit and honnesty. I wan't the opportunity to grow with the company evolving with the project I lead and have the opportunity to developp new skills. I need to be aligned with the company project.
